My Resume
  • Hi, I’m Tien, but you can call me Sussy Shrimp. Welcome to MUSE PLAYBOX, my personal portfolio.

MUSE PLAYBOX emerges from a whimsical collection of the muse - the creative inspirations drawn from my experience as a musician, a writer, and a culture explorer. Those inspirations eventually seeped into my personal life, shaping my artistic perspective, and through a lot of musing, manifested in the final forms of personal creative artworks. MUSE PLAYBOX is not only a museum of my creations but also a playful diary of my ongoing artistic journey. 

  • Music

As a musician, I have performed in various concerts as well as participated in prestigious competitions both in Vietnam and overseas. However, the most memorable experience to me is organizing a concert myself. Euphoria concert 2024 honed my pianist skills and taught me how to use music power to help the community. We have donated all the money from our campaign to UNICEF and also support children from SOS Village with the facilities we received. 

  • Drama and Writing

The passion for theater motivates me to try out different roles in a stage performance. From an actor to a playwright, from standing straight on the stage to planning every scene behind the curtains, all of them fascinate me.

My love for music and my passion for culture have united, forming my identity as a culture explorer who embraces it all.
