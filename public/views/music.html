This competition took place in the COVID-19 pandemic. It was a hard year for me, because everything had to be online. Fortunately, my performance still impressed the judges and they gave my the first prize.

Grande Brilliante Valse Chopin

My favourite piece. I played it in my charitable concert, Euphoria 2024, as well as some other competitions. A quick playthrough of this waltz is the perfect way to unwind and escape the burdens of everyday life.

Prelude 2 NGUYEN DINH LUONG

A classical piece from a Vietnamese author. It's the perfect blend between traditional music - as can be seen from the pentatonic scales - and classical music theory. It resonates with me personally, reflecting a harmony of diverse cultural influences.

Euphoria Concert 2024

However, what I appreciate the most throughout my adventure with the piano is the chance to spread love and kindness using the power of music. Organizing the charitable Euphoria Concert 2024 myself, I deepened my understanding of music's power to uplift and support the community. Through our campaign, we were able to donate all the proceeds to UNICEF, making a meaningful impact on children's lives. Additionally, we provided essential facilities for children at Hanoi SOS Village, reinforcing our commitment to giving back and using our talents for a greater purpose.
